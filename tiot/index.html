PoSy Pools act as decentralized access gateways into the SEALCOIN network. They provide:

  • Secure Onboarding for devices using locked TIOT tokens
  • Incentive Structures through transaction-based rewards
  • KYO (Know Your Object) mechanisms for regulated industries
  • Penalty Enforcement for bad actors (malicious devices lose staked tokens)

Example: A utility company can create a private PoSy pool to onboard thousands of certified smart meters and enforce custom rules.

Tokenomics Snapshot

  • Symbol: TIOT
  • Total Supply: 10,000,000,000 (fixed, no minting)
  • Initial Price: $0.03 USD
  • Utility + Payment Token: Not an asset/security token
  • Emission Strategy: Controlled sale + vesting + tiered participation

To prevent oversupply and price volatility, SEALCOIN AG will implement a structured release schedule. The cliff and vesting periods for founders, team members, investors, and advisors ensure that tokens are gradually unlocked over time, supporting the long-term stability and growth of the SEALCOIN ecosystem.

Device-to-Device Transactions

SEALCOIN platform facilitates automated transactions between IoT devices, where devices independently negotiate, execute, and settle service-for-payment exchanges without human intervention.

For example, a drone identifies a low battery, autonomously finds a nearby charging station within the SEALCOIN network, and completes a secure, automated payment and recharge process.

Secure Energy Trading in Smart Grids

Within smart grids, SEALCOIN platform enables peer-to-peer energy trading. Homeowners with renewable energy sources, like solar panels, can directly sell excess energy to other consumers or devices in real-time.

This system allows homeowners to act as independent energy producers and sellers, contributing to a decentralized energy market and enhancing grid flexibility.

Automated Supply Chain Management

SEALCOIN's platform supports a decentralized, automated solution for managing supply chain processes. By integrating smart contracts, the platform enables secure, real-time transactions between suppliers, manufacturers, and other stakeholders, minimizing manual intervention.

This streamlines inventory monitoring, order fulfillment, and payment processing, reducing errors and enhancing supply chain resilience.

Decentralized Data Marketplace

SEALCOIN enables a decentralized data marketplace where IoT devices autonomously exchange verified, high-value data secured by post-quantum cryptography. Devices act as data publishers, earning tokens for sharing accurate telemetry or sensor information. In industries like agriculture, energy, and logistics, this system turns trustworthy, tamper-proof data into a valuable currency, creating self-sustaining, secure IoT ecosystems across borders and connectivity gaps.

Data Oracle

SEALCOIN acts as a trusted data oracle, delivering real-time, verified IoT data to smart contracts and decentralized applications across blockchains, ensuring tamper-proof inputs for automation and logic execution.

Accurate on-chain data is critical for automation. SEALCOIN ensures reliability by aggregating and securing IoT data at the edge, enabling smarter, real-world-connected decentralized applications in finance, energy, and logistics.

Proof-of-concept

Each IoT device registers on SEALCOIN platform with a unique digital ID, secured by encrypted authentication, ensuring trusted and autonomous operations. Using the TIOT token, devices can independently initiate transactions, share data, and engage in secure service-for-payment exchanges without intermediaries.

In July 2024, SEALCOIN successfully executed a Proof of Concept, demonstrating the platform's capability to facilitate secure, real-time IoT transactions. This PoC highlights SEALCOIN's foundational role in building a decentralized IoT ecosystem.
